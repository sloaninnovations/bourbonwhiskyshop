Top 100 Bourbons of 2022

World's Top 100 Best Bourbon Brands A kind of American whiskey mainly derived from maize, bourbon is a distilled spirit that has been matured in barrels. The distillation of bourbon dates back to the 18th century. It wasn't until the 1850s that the term "Bourbon" was used. It wasn't until the 1870s that the Kentucky etymology was developed. Although bourbon may be produced anywhere in the United States, Kentucky and the American South are where it is most often found. Wikipedia.
